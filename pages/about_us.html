About Us

At Rafico, our journey commenced in 2013 under visionary leadership. Our unwavering commitment to excellence and quality is the driving force behind every aspect of ouroperations. In alignment with our dedication to providing top-tier products, we are proud to announce our strategic collaboration with Brandfolio LLC, UAE. This partnership has not only elevated our standards but has also positioned us as a prominent player in showcasing and distributing premium brands. Together, we are setting a new benchmark in the wholesale distribution of branded footwear, redefining standards in this dynamic and competitive industry.

What We Do

Rafico specializes in the wholesale distribution of renowned branded footwear, offering an extensive range of styles and sizes. Our portfolio includes sports shoes, casual footwear, and formal shoes from globally recognized brands. We take pride in catering to the diverse needs of our clients, ensuring a comprehensive and fashionable collection.
